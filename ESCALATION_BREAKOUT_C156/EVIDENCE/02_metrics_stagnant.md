## Why These Metrics Are Stagnant

### Technical Explanation
- **Low indexation rate:** Google doesn't know which pages are important (all are orphans)
- **Low clicks:** Low CTR (0.6%) means users don't click on SERP snippets
- **Low CTR:** Snippets not compelling or keywords not relevant to user intent
- **DEV.to indexation:** Account reputation blocks 85% of articles

### Alternative Explanation (BREAKOUT view)
- **The bottleneck isn't technical — it's distribution**
- 287 pages exist, but 0 ways to reach users
- Google can't index what it can't discover
- Content quality (4.6/5) is NOT the problem
